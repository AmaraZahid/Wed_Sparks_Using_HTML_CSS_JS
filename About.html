AI-Based Search and Filtering

Find the Perfect Services with AI

Our web app utilizes advanced AI technology to provide a seamless and personalized search experience for our users. With AI-based search and filtering capabilities, finding the services that meet your needs has never been easier.

Search and Filter Based on Your Preferences

Users can search for services based on various preferences such as location, price range, ratings, and more. Our intelligent AI algorithms analyze and understand your search queries to provide accurate and relevant results.

Discover Services Tailored to You

Our AI-powered recommendation system takes into account your preferences and previous interactions to suggest services that align with your unique needs. Whether you're looking for wedding venues, photographers, or caterers, our system ensures you discover the perfect options.

Roles and Dashboard

Different Roles in the Web App

Our web app offers different roles to cater to various users. These roles include Super Admin, Customer Representative, and Finance. Each role has specific permissions and responsibilities within the platform.

Super Admin

The Super Admin has the highest level of access and control. They can create and manage different roles, set permissions, and oversee the overall functionality of the web app.

Customer Representative

The Customer Representative role is responsible for assisting customers, addressing their queries, and providing support throughout their wedding planning journey.

Finance

The Finance role handles financial aspects, such as invoicing, payment processing, and financial reporting within the web app.
